Photo Gallery: National Landmarks

This is a view of the famous summer destination Cape Cod in Massachusetts.
It sits of soft, white sand and next to striking blue water. You can take walks and
bike around the coast or tour the historic lighthouses.

The Golden Gate Bridge is an iconic suspension bridge in an unmistakable
red color. It stretches over waters connecting the San Fran Bay and the Pacific Ocean.

This piece of American history has become the symbol of North America. It sits
in New York and is one of the best known tourist attractions in the world. The statue stands
305 feet with her right arm high in the air.

Yosemite is a beautiful national park in California's Sierra Nevada mountains. It was
protected in 1864 and has become one of the most well known historical landmarks. It is
known for its beautiful mountains and waterfalls.

The Korean War Memorial is with many other historical landmarks in Washington D.C. Its
construction started in the early 90's and was completed in 1995. The memorial consists of
a mural wall, 19 haunting stainless steel statues, a pool of remembrance, and a united nations wall.
